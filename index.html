Services

Why Adhija Home Care is Different?

"Adhija is led by highly skilled ICU trained staffs. At Adhija, we bridge the gap between the Hospital ICU and your Home through Doctor-led protocols and ICU-trained staff."

1. Advanced Respiratory Support

  • BiPAP & CPAP Management: Expertise in non-invasive ventilation (NIV) for COPD, Sleep Apnea, and Post-ICU weaning. We don't just provide the machine; we ensure correct pressure settings and mask-fitment under specialist supervision.
  • High-Flow Oxygen Therapy: Clinical-grade oxygen concentrators and cylinder support for chronic lung conditions (ILD, Fibrosis) and palliative care.
  • SpO2 Monitoring & Titration: Professional guidance on adjusting oxygen flow based on patient requirements to prevent oxygen toxicity or hypoxia.

2. Critical Care Nursing (Home ICU)

  • Post-Hospitalization Transition: Specialized care for patients recently discharged from the ICU or High Dependency Unit (HDU).
  • Tracheostomy & Stoma Care: Professional cleaning, suctioning, and management of tracheostomy tubes to prevent infections.
  • ICD & Drain Management: Post-surgical care and monitoring of chest drains and catheters by ICU-trained nurses.

3. Specialized Medical Procedures

  • Ryles Tube & Catheterization: Sterile insertion and management of feeding tubes and urinary catheters.
  • Bed-Sore (Pressure Injury) Care: Advanced wound dressing and specialized air-bed setups to treat and prevent stage 1-4 pressure ulcers.
  • Infusion Therapy: Administration of IV fluids, antibiotics, and injections as per hospital protocols.

4. Rehabilitation & Elderly Care

  • Chest Physiotherapy: Specialized lung expansion exercises and mucus clearance techniques for bedbound patients.
  • Neuro-Rehab: Focused physiotherapy for post-stroke recovery and mobility restoration.
  • Elderly Wellness: Holistic 24/7 or 12/7 nursing support for the elderly, focusing on medication adherence and fall prevention.
